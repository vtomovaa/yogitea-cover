Our Story

Ayurveda is an ancient healing philosophy that takes a holistic approach to wellness, emphasizing the importance of bringing body, mind, and spirit into a state of balance. This philosophy informs how we develop our teas and our approach to the world.

Rooted in Ayurveda

Over the past five decades, we’ve made delicious, functional teas inspired by the ancient holistic healing philosophy of Ayurveda. We’ve also expanded to address specific needs with specialized herbal formulas blended for both delicious taste and healthful benefit.
Today, YOGI TEA® and CHOICE® have over 50 tea blends made from ethically-sourced 100 exotic spices and botanicals. We believe in the synergistic wellness benefits of herbs and complementing them with intriguing, delightful flavors – qualities that make all of our teas uniquely Yogi.

Our Process

Creating naturally delicious, high-quality teas.

We use the highest quality teas, spices and botanicals for their natural goodness. With high sourcing standards, rigorous testing, and a commitment to creating products that minimize our environmental footprint, we create delicious teas for you while respecting our planet.

Responsible Sourcing

We are extremely selective with our suppliers and require that they avoid mineral nitrogen fertilizers and synthetic chemical pesticides and perform annual audits by the national partners of the internationally active certification bodies Ecocert and IMO. The impeccable quality of our products is our top priority. To ensure that it is maintained throughout the entire supply chain, we have our teas checked at all important points of production in accordance with the so-called HACCP guideline for food hygiene (Hazard Analysis Critical Control Points).

Ingredients Standards and Quality Testing

We test all of our ingredients to validate that each one meets our high standards for color, aroma, and taste. These tests include (among others) microbiology testing, identity confirmation, sensory evaluations, and chromatography techniques. This quality-assurance testing ensures the use of quality ingredients in all YOGI TEA® and CHOICE® teas, cup after cup after cup.

Tea Packaging

Our tea bag filtration paper is made from a blend of high quality manila hemp (abaca) fibers and wood pulp, which is free from plastics and oxygen bleached a natural process that s free of chemicals or toxins. Along with all other components of our packaging, this paper is free of gluten, GMOs, and corn.

Our Purpose

Supporting quality, sustainability and social responsibility.

We are in This Together

Our goal is to make every place we touch better. We believe in using our power as a business to foster global well-being. Guided by that purpose, we support a variety of meaningful projects that promote well-being around the world. We focus our support on local projects with global impact.

Do it Right . Then do it Better

Since day one, YOGI TEA® has been about doing what’s right. At the same time, we know we can always be better. That’s why we’re always working to improve the way we do things. It’s this mindset that’s led us to create tea bags that are compostable, tea cartons that are made from 100% FSC® – certified paper and use climate-neutral shipping for our ingredients. It’s also led us to work with anyone, even other tea companies, because here it’s not about competing to increase profits, it’s about collaborating to help us, our industry, and the world, be better.

Move Beyond Expectation

We’re expected to make deliciously purposeful tea. And that’s important to us. But we want to do so much more than what is expected. We’re a certified B-Corp®, proving that our business can thrive while putting people before profit. All the ingredients for YOGI TEA® are sourced 100% from controlled ecological farms and are therefore certified organic. We’re part of the Ethical Tea Partnership, the world’s leading organisation for promoting sustainability in the tea industry. It’s more than tea. It’s more than certifications. It’s creating a better world by going beyond what’s expected.

Ingredients

Cinnamon

Cinnamon is among the most expensive spices in the world and was supposedly already used as a spice in China in 3,000 B.C. Cinnamon is extracted from the bark of the South-Asian cinnamon tree. It has an aromatic-sweetish taste and contains valuable essential oils.

Ginger

Whether in the Christmas biscuits, as a curry mixture or in lemonade: The bulbous ginger is among the best-known spice plants in the world. For thousands of years, it has been cultivated in the tropical heat of eastern Asia. It gives many of our YOGI TEA®s a fruity-hot and aromatically spicy taste.

Green tea - Sencha

Our sencha tea is sourced from a tea garden run by Hunan Tea Company Limited of China. The tea leaves are picked by small-scale farmers on their organic farms and have an extremely mellow, balanced flavour.
